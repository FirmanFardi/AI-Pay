Development Milestones
Scope: FPX Payments (Single Sponsoring Bank) – 12 Month Roadmap
0 / 12 Milestones Completed (0%)

Month 1 – Platform Foundation

  • Architecture design for modular payments platform
  • User authentication & Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
  • Merchant onboarding workflows with sandbox data
  • Database schema design and secure storage encryption (AES-256)

Month 2 – Compliance & Risk Controls

  • Implement PCI DSS v4.0 compliance controls (encryption, tokenization, logging)
  • KYC/KYB verification workflow with fraud scoring
  • AML monitoring rules & transaction anomaly detection
  • Centralized tamper-proof audit logging

Month 3 – Bank Engagement & Architecture Review

  • Sponsoring bank application submission and required documentation
  • Provide technical architecture, security diagrams, and compliance evidence
  • Conduct threat modeling & security risk assessment for FPX flows

Month 4 – FPX Sandbox Integration

  • Integration with FPX sandbox APIs (requests, callbacks, status checks)
  • Implement idempotency and transaction state management
  • Logging & alerting for failed transactions or timeouts

Month 5 – Extended Sandbox Testing

  • Edge case testing: network failures, double-spend attempts, payment reversal
  • Load testing to simulate high TPS (Transactions Per Second)
  • Reconciliation testing against sandbox reports
  • Automated regression tests for all critical flows

Month 6 – FPX Certification (UAT)

  • Execute FPX test cases & obtain bank UAT approval
  • Bug fixes from certification feedback
  • Penetration testing (OWASP Top 10) and mitigation of findings
  • Record test evidence for audit and bank submission

Month 7 – Pre-Production Hardening

  • Security hardening: WAF, HTTPS/TLS 1.3, firewall rules
  • Performance tuning and caching strategies
  • 24/7 system monitoring with alert escalation
  • Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for incidents & rollback

Month 8 – Bank Approval & Production Setup

  • Obtain final bank sign-off and production credentials
  • Deploy FPX to production environment with full logging
  • Enable real-time transaction monitoring dashboards
  • Validate integration with internal reconciliation engine

Month 9 – Pilot Merchant Onboarding

  • Onboard small group of pilot merchants (low-volume)
  • 24/7 transaction monitoring with anomaly detection
  • Daily automated reconciliation reports
  • Capture merchant feedback and iterate

Month 10 – Extended Pilot & Monitoring

  • Gradually increase pilot merchant volume
  • Real-time fraud detection & automated alerts
  • API performance monitoring & detailed logs
  • Continuous merchant support & incident resolution

Month 11 – Stabilization & Audit Readiness

  • Internal audit for PCI DSS, AML, and KYC compliance
  • Bug fixes & platform optimization
  • Finalize operational procedures & escalation matrix
  • Prepare compliance evidence for regulators and bank audits

Month 12 – Limited Commercial Launch

  • Gradual public rollout with controlled transaction volume
  • 24/7 operational monitoring with automated alerts
  • Incident response readiness and SLA tracking
  • Continuous optimization and reporting to stakeholders
Important Notes:
  • FPX availability is subject to bank approval and certification.
  • Timeline includes extended testing and security hardening to reduce operational risk.
  • Scope excludes e-wallets and credit card processing.
